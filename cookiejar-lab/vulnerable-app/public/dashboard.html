🔍 Verify It Yourself in DevTools

Open your browser's developer tools to see the vulnerability firsthand:

  1. Press F12 (or Cmd+Opt+I on Mac) to open DevTools
  2. Go to the Application tab (Chrome) or Storage tab (Firefox)
  3. Expand Cookies and click your site's URL
  4. Find the session cookie - notice HttpOnly is unchecked
  5. Now switch to the Console tab and type: document.cookie
  6. You'll see the full JWT - this is exactly what an infostealer reads
