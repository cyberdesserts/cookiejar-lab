🔒 HttpOnly Cookie Demo

Try reading the session cookie with JavaScript - it's not accessible:

document.cookie

Result:

In the vulnerable app (port 3001), document.cookie returns the full JWT. Here, httpOnly prevents that entirely - an infostealer using JS injection gets nothing.

🔍 Verify in DevTools: Press F12 → Application → Cookies → click your site's URL. Find the session cookie - notice HttpOnly is checked. Now open the vulnerable app in another tab and compare: its session cookie has HttpOnly unchecked.
